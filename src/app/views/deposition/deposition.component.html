What is PEDdeposition service?

PEDdeposition website is a tool that enables users to deposit the structural ensembles of proteins. Within the website you are able to define detailed descriptions about the experimental procedures and the computational methods used for the generation of conformational ensembles. Moreover, there is a possibility to cross-reference to other experimental and protein databases and add information about the expression organism.

One of the major features of the PEDdeposition tool is applying an automated pipeline to compute various characteristics of the ensembles such as, radius of gyration, secondary structural elements and relative solvent accessibility. Finally, after submitting an ensemble on the web server, our curators will be reviewing the information and the deposited entry will be published in the next release of Protein Ensemble Database (PED).
